Game Instruction

  1. Hit "Start Game" button to launch the game.
  2. The same button will be used for "Pause" and "Resume Game"
  3. Blue Box is the Snake Body
  4. Green Box is the Snake Food
  5. Yellow Box is the Bonus Food
  6. Red Box is the Wall Block
  7. Snake Food gives 1 Point
  8. Bonus Food gives 5 Point
  9. Avoid the "wall" and "wall blocks"
  10. Complete the required score to move to next level
  11. Best of Luck!!!
